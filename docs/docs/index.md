The NTP sandbox is a collection of developer environments which are used for hosting multiple NTP applications for different projects; generally (but not always) in the computational-toxicology realm of research at the NTP. This environment is  designed to be an area for rapid development and prototyping of new ideas; if they are valuable then the projects may be expanded and re-factored for implementation elsewhere such as CEBS.

The NTP sandbox is deployed on two servers, a public-facing production server:

- Production (public URL): [https://sandbox.ntp.niehs.nih.gov](https://sandbox.ntp.niehs.nih.gov)

And an internal staging-server:

- Staging (NIH VPN only): [https://sandbox-staging.ntp.niehs.nih.gov](https://sandbox-staging.ntp.niehs.nih.gov)
